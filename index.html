What is WPT?

Wireless Power Technology moves energy without physical conductors.

Wireless Power Technology uses electromagnetic waves to transmit energy across distance. In microwave WPT, electricity is converted into radio-frequency power, directed through antennas or phased arrays, captured by a receiver, and converted back into usable electricity.

Its long-term potential includes power delivery to remote sites, drones, satellites, lunar infrastructure, disaster zones, and possibly space-based solar power. The central challenge is not whether power can be beamed — it is whether large systems can be made efficient, safe, scalable, and economically useful.
