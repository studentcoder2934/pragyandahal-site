About

Energy should be abundant, reliable, and accessible.

Hello, my name is Pragyan Dahal. I was born in Nepal and grew up in the Dallas, Texas area. I went to high school at L. D. Bell High School in Hurst, Texas, and I am currently attending Stanford University, majoring in Energy Science and Engineering.

I am interested in grid technology, energy production, and energy distribution. I believe energy is a right, not a privilege. Every person, entity, and business should be able to access the energy they need at low cost, with reliability and convenience.
